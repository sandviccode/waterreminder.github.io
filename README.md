# Data Collection
Water Reminder app only stores data locally on your device. No personal information is sent to our servers or third-party services.

# Data Usage
Collected data (water consumption logs, reminder settings, personalization preferences) is only used to provide app functionality. This data never leaves your device.

# Data Storage
All your data is stored in your device\'s local storage (AsyncStorage). When you uninstall the app, all your data is automatically deleted.

# Data Sharing
No data is shared, sold, or used for advertising purposes with third parties. Your data belongs entirely to you and is only under your control.

# User Rights
You have full control over your data at all times. You can permanently delete all your data by uninstalling the app.
