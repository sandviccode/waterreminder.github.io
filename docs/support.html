How to Use

Getting Started

1. Set Your Goal: Go to Settings and set your daily water intake goal based on your needs.

2. Log Your Water: Tap the water button on the home screen each time you drink water.

3. Track Progress: Watch your circular progress indicator fill up as you reach your daily goal.

4. View History: Check the History tab to see your past hydration records.

Settings

  • Daily Goal: Adjust your target water intake (in ml)
  • Notifications: Enable/disable hydration reminders
  • Reminder Interval: Set how often you want to be reminded
  • Language: Choose between English and Turkish
  • Theme: Switch between light and dark mode

Notifications

The app sends gentle reminders throughout the day to help you stay hydrated. You can customize the reminder frequency in the Settings menu.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much water should I drink daily?

While individual needs vary, a general recommendation is 2000-3000ml (8-12 cups) per day. Factors like activity level, climate, and health conditions can affect your needs. Consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

Can I customize the water amount per serving?

Yes! You can adjust the amount added with each tap in the Settings menu to match your drinking habits (e.g., 250ml for a glass, 500ml for a bottle).

Will my data be saved?

Yes, all your hydration data is stored locally on your device and persists between app sessions.

Does the app work offline?

Absolutely! Water Reminder works completely offline. No internet connection is required.

How do I reset my daily progress?

Your progress automatically resets at midnight each day. You can also manually reset from the Settings menu if needed.

Tips for Staying Hydrated

  • Start your day with a glass of water
  • Keep a water bottle with you throughout the day
  • Drink water before, during, and after exercise
  • Set reminders to drink water regularly
  • Eat water-rich foods like fruits and vegetables
  • Listen to your body - thirst is a sign you need water
