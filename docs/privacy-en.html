Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

By using the Water Reminder app, you agree to this privacy policy. This policy explains how your data is collected, used, and protected.

Data Collection

Water Reminder app only stores data locally on your device. No personal information is sent to our servers or third-party services.

The app stores the following data locally:

  • Your water consumption logs (date, time, amount)
  • Your reminder settings (frequency, silent hours)
  • Your personal preferences (daily goal, cup size, language, theme)
  • Statistical data (daily/weekly/monthly consumption)

Data Usage

Collected data (water consumption logs, reminder settings, personalization preferences) is only used to provide app functionality. This data never leaves your device.

Your data is used for:

  • Tracking your water intake and generating statistics
  • Sending you personalized reminders
  • Customizing your app experience
  • Helping you reach your daily goals

Data Storage

All your data is stored in your device's local storage (AsyncStorage). When you uninstall the app, all your data is automatically deleted.

Your data:

  • Exists only on your device
  • Doesn't require internet connection
  • Is not uploaded to cloud servers
  • Is stored unencrypted locally (protected by your device's own security measures)

Data Sharing

No data is shared, sold, or used for advertising purposes with third parties. Your data belongs entirely to you and is only under your control.

We absolutely:

  • Do not sell your data to third-party companies
  • Do not share with advertising networks
  • Do not send to analytics services
  • Do not upload to any servers

Notifications

The app uses local notifications to remind you to drink water. These notifications:

  • Are generated entirely on your device
  • Don't require internet connection
  • Don't send or receive any data
  • Can be customized according to your settings
  • Can be disabled at any time

User Rights

You have full control over your data at all times. You can permanently delete all your data by uninstalling the app.

Your rights:

  • Access your data at any time
  • Delete all your data from app settings
  • Permanently delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Contact us with concerns about data collection

Third-Party Services

Water Reminder does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data collection tools. The app is completely independent and runs only on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all age groups. Parental consent is recommended for children under 13. Since no personal information is collected, no extra precautions are needed for children's privacy.

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you within the app when there are significant changes. You can follow policy changes on this page.
